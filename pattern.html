Zen Garden Style-guide

Theme: Forest & Mountains

Our theme will include a color scheme that uses various greens to reflect the outdoors. Texture swatches and background images of wood or rock may be used. Scrolling feature that continues a visual of the outdoors, horizontal for larger view, and vertical for mobile view.
